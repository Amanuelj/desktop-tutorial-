FAQ

What kinds of sessions do you offer?

Amanu Visuals offers photography for weddings, maternity, newborn, portraits, branding, interiors, boudoir, graduation, fashion, pets, cars, architecture, products, couples, family, and more.

How do I book?

Fill out the inquiry form with your session type, preferred date, location, and vision. From there, the details can be discussed and confirmed directly.

Can I choose my location and style?

Yes. Sessions can be tailored around your preferred mood, setting, wardrobe ideas, and creative direction to match the final look you want.

How long does it take to receive my photos?

Most portrait sessions are delivered within 1–2 weeks. Larger events and weddings may require additional editing time.

Do you help with posing?

Absolutely. Every session includes natural posing guidance to help you feel comfortable, confident, and look your best.
